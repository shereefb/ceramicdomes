FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What sizes do the zomes come in?

You can have any size you want as long as it's 19 feet in diamter (300 square foot floor space). We're just getting started, and will introduce different size in the future. 

Do I need a foundation?

No. Your Zome come with a pre-cast foundation that bolts together in a couple of hours. All you need is a level site with a few inches of gravel for good drainage. 

If you already have a level foundation, our domes can be installed on it. 

Where are you materials sourced?

Short answer: As locally as possible. The perlite is mined right next door. The Magnesia is from Michigan, and the EPS is US based. Our wood panels come from lumber harvested from renewable, local sources. 

How do the panels connect?

We designed a tongue and groove system that keeps our panel super snug. The overlapping design and rubber gaskets keep water away, and seal the interior. It's really quite a clever design, and we're pretty proud of it. 

Can I paint the interior?

Yes. We think it would be a shame though. The wood is really pretty.  You can paint the exterior as well if you want. 

What maintenance does it need?

Pretty much no maintenance is needed.  Rodents and insects are not interested in ceramics or the foam. Mold doesn't grow in the walls. Moisture has little to no impact. The shell is tough and doesn't settle or warp. 

How thick are the walls?

Five inches.

What about plumbing?

Sorry. No built-in plumbing yet. You're going to need to call your local guy. 

How many pounds can it withstand if it snows?

A rule of thumb one inch of heavy, wet snow weighs 1.66 pounds per square foot. Our panels have a compression strength of 2000 pounds per square inch. So, they'll start to fail if you get about 15,000 feet of snow. At this point though, you have bigger problems than a crushed zome. 

How do you heat/cool?

For heating, space heaters or a gas/wood stove (if you plan on putting in a stove, ask us for a chimney panel to make things easier). The high ceiling and operable windows make cooling easier. 

Is there custom made furniture?

Glad you asked. Yes! We plan to offer shelves, cabinets, bed headboards, and desks that fit perfectly agains our walls. Stay tuned. 

How long does it take to assemble?

Our Zomes were designed to be assembled by a couple of handy people and a scaffold in a weekend.

How soon can I ship it?

Soon! We're processing pre-orders right now, and expect to ship out your Zome within 15 days of your order being placed. 

Where are you based?

We hand-build our Zomes in a small warehouse factory in down town Petaluma, California.

What about electricity?

Your Zome comes pre-wired with electrical outlets. Just plug and play. 

How do I repair the surface if something hits it?

The ceramic cement shell is tough. But things happen. A rogue baseball won't hurt, but a big tree limb could crack it. This doesn't impact the structural integrity of the Zome since it is mesh enforced. However, the cracks can be repaired swiftly by patching . If you need it, we will ship you a bucket of our bio-ceramic cement that you can simply mix with water and apply to the crack to re-seal it. If worse comes to worst, the panels can be individually replaced. 

Once it's installed, can it be moved?

We designed our zomes to be dis-assembled and moved if needed. Nothing in the assembly is nailed, glued or grouted. Everything comes together with bolts, screws, and tongue and groove. 

What's the R value of the insulation?

The walls are rated R-20. Which is great, but it's only one dimension of thermal insulation. The air-tightness, thermal mass, and shape all contribute to our Zomes being thermally superior to traditional building. 

Is it water proof?

Yes. We initially wanted to make Goedesic domes but it turns out they are notoriously leaky. So we scratched the design and built our zomes from the ground up to create a lapped connection between the panels. This, along with our air-tight rubber seal makes sure water stays where it should. Outside.

What about the floor?

Our zomes don't come with a floor. Our foundation, however, comes with pre-built  joist hangers for a floor system. We also send you what you need to built your floor: shopping list with exact lumber cuts, plans, tools and building instructions. A Home Depot run, and a dedicated weekend should get the job done. 

Can I hang things on the walls / ceiling?

Yes. Each panel has an internal "nailer" frame that's 1.5 inches thick. That's how the interior wood panels are attached. You can use this frame to hang shelves, pictures, dream catchers...or anything else you like. 

Is this a fire shelter?

No. Legally we can't recommend you shelter in our Zomes during a fire. In case of a fire, you should evacuate immediately and follow local authorities recommendations. What we can tell you is that we keep our photo albums and favorite stuffed animals in our Zome, and if we were stuck in a fire and couldn't evacuate, we would shelter there with our kids and pets.
